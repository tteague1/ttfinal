Event Safe

About

This app will allow you to keep track of the children coming and going from your event. By using this app, you will have a picture of the child with the time in and time out of the event. You can also enter the name of the person that dropped the child off or brought them to the event.

The main goal here is to assist you in keeping the children of your event safe. By using the tabs at the bottom, you can start adding event info, child information and then see a final screen with the information entered.

Take a look at the src/pages/ directory to add or change tabs, update any existing page or create new pages.
